Artist Biography

S.O.N (Something Outta Nothing) is a horrorcore rapper out of Sharon, PA who built his name from the ground up under the alias Scrub King before rebranding with a sound that goes darker and deeper. Rooted in classic boom-bap production, S.O.N layers gritty, confrontational lyricism with themes of ritualistic black magic and occult energy, a combination that's earned him a loyal following in the underground horrorcore community. His hustle goes back to at least 2011, when he dropped his Scrub King Mixtape, followed by D.U.I. (Dying Under the Influence) and a collab project in 2012. After years grinding in the underground, he returned in 2021 with Grant Us Eyes as S.O.N, and dropped The Bastard Son in 2025. In June 2023, he was officially signed to Long Live Evil (LLE) at Camp Xul 3, cementing his place in one of horrorcore's most active rosters. S.O.N has shared stages with major names across the horrorcore scene and built a reputation through years of collaboration and touring in the underground. Something outta nothing, indeed.
