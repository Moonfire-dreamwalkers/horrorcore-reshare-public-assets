Artist Biography

Klokwerk E is a 5th Dimensional Hip Hop duo out of Columbus, Ohio, made up of Billy D and Kyle Kombs, better known together as the Silver Boof Brothers. Their sound is hard to pin down in the best way, blending underground hip-hop with dark, absurdist, and conceptual themes while keeping things deeply human at their core. Kyle Kombs handles production, and his beats have steadily evolved with each release, while Billy D brings the lyrical energy and personality that makes the duo click. What sets them apart is the range they manage to pull off: their catalog can go from grim and heavy to genuinely heartfelt, often within the same project. Since debuting with Will Boof 4 Rent in 2023, they have moved quickly, dropping the full-length The Emerald Tablets, the acclaimed Anubis, and multiple EPs in 2025 including Bukkake Mukbang Extravaganza and Rent a Friend. They have also formed the supergroup Mighty Morphin' Wackness alongside the Super Famous Fun Time Guys. Nobody outwerks the KLOK.
