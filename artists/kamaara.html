Artist Biography

Kamaara is a rapper, producer, and artist out of Fort Lauderdale, Florida, now based in North Carolina. His sound is rooted in trap metal and industrial hip-hop, built on dark, heavy beats and an aggressive delivery that has been his signature since his debut in 2017. His solo discography runs from Valley (2017) through Next Time You See Me I'll Be Dead on Your Doorstep || (2018), Cowboy Cutie (2019), and TrenchBoy|| (2020), each project pushing further into experimental and atmospheric territory. Beyond his solo work he is also a member of the collective Haunted House, and has a collab project with Savage Ga$p as well as Circling Terror with Kill Ebola in 2025. He produces much of his own material and has built a reputation as a tireless collaborator in the dark underground rap scene. With active touring across the US and Europe, Kamaara has steadily grown from a regional underground name into a genuinely international presence.
