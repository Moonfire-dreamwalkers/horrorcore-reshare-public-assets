Artist Biography

Kill Dyll is a Denver-based trap metal artist, producer, and visual creator who has built one of the more distinctive identities in the underground scene. Starting out as a drummer, he eventually began producing his own beats, rapping over them, and shaping a persona he describes as an artistic interpretation of ego in America. The result is a sound rooted in heavy bass, distorted vocals, and confrontational energy that pulls from hip-hop, metal, rock, and industrial. Everything about the project is self-contained. He produces his own music, directs his own videos, and makes his own handmade masks, the horror-themed face coverings that have become his calling card. That contrast between the unsettling visuals and his more reserved off-stage personality is a big part of what resonates with his fanbase. His discography moved fast once things clicked. sixty se7en and JFK in 2022, dont touch me. in 2023, and a run of releases in 2024 including Antagonist, EMBRACE THE CHAOS VOL.1, and the Warner Records-backed mixtape BRAINDEAD. Most recently he dropped ACT HUMAN, not god in 2025. With a rapidly growing fanbase and major label backing already in the picture, Kill Dyll is one of the scene's fastest rising names.
