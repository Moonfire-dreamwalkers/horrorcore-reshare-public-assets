Artist Biography

GODHANDUSA is an aggressive underground duo out of California, made up of Gonda and Mongo, two aliases adopted specifically for this project. Part of the Spider Gang collective, the duo channel a sound rooted in Memphis rap and cloud rap, filtered through a distinctive 8-bit vocal effect that gives their music an immediately recognizable, almost lo-fi video game quality. Since launching in 2024 with GODHAND VOLUME 0 and their debut proper GODHAND VOLUME 1, they have moved fast, dropping GODHAND VOLUME 2 and the mixtape IGNORANCE IS BLISS in 2025, followed by GODHAND VOLUME 3 in 2026. The project has a loose, high-energy feel, equal parts fun and unhinged, with the duo leaning into the chaos rather than away from it.
