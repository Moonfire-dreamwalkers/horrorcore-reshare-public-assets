Artist Biography

BatiBatt is a gothic horror artist out of West Chester, Pennsylvania who has been on a warpath since stepping into the underground scene in 2021. Her signature is a dynamic vocal range that moves fluidly between soft and ethereal to devastating screams, all wrapped in witchy symbolism, occult-themed visuals, and deeply personal themes of self-love, death, revenge, and spiritual awakening. Her debut Virago (2021) came out swinging across eight tracks of trap metal and industrial-influenced aggression, built on psychological turmoil, horror imagery, and demonology. Three years later, Fall From Grace (2024) showed a clear evolution. Still eight tracks, but leaning further into post-metal, melodic singing, and dark pop, expanding her range without softening her edge. The themes of demon lore, human psychology, and introspective self-reflection hit harder for the added dimension, and the artwork, a veiled figure with dark hands covering the face and a crown of thorns dripping blood, is among the most striking in the underground. She followed it up with two singles in 2025, Broken Garden and Wrath, making clear she has no intention of slowing down.
