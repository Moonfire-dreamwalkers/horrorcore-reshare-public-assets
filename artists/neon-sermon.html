Artist Biography

Neon Sermon is a horrorcore supergroup made up of Philadelphia veteran Lo Key, BatiBatt, and KruX 1, three underground artists who came together with a concept as bold as their sound: an alien collective from the distant planetary system of Cosmosis, arriving on Earth to infiltrate human consciousness through weaponized sound and psychoactive frequencies. They are, according to them, 'definitely NOT plotting world domination.' Central to the group's mythology is Kaos, a mysterious green liquid that symbolizes their power to transform everything it touches. The aesthetic leans hard into neon-drenched, LED-lit sci-fi visuals that look like a rave on a spaceship designed by someone who has fully committed to the bit, blending futuristic alien-hybrid imagery with dark mechanical energy. Musically they hit hard, fusing rapid-fire rap with aggressive electronic music across hardcore rap, EDM, dubstep, and industrial textures, all layered with sci-fi soundscapes and themes bouncing between alien invasion, psychological transformation, party culture, and full-blown cosmic mythology. Their debut 001 dropped in 2022 with 8 tracks of relentless, grimy, high-energy sound that pushes horrorcore into genuinely new sonic territory. One note from the recording sessions that has become something of legend: don't eat the props. The full story remains unclear. Some things are better left a mystery.
