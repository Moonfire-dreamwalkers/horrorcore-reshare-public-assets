Why Audit Your Artist Page?

A well-optimized artist page loads faster, ranks higher in search results, and provides a better experience for fans discovering your music. Page audits check for common issues like slow-loading images, missing meta tags, mobile usability problems, and accessibility barriers that can keep people from finding your content.

  • Performance: How fast does your page load on mobile and desktop?
  • SEO: Is your page structured so search engines can properly index it?
  • Accessibility: Can all fans, including those using screen readers, navigate your page?
  • Best Practices: Does your page follow modern web standards?

How It Works

The audit runs entirely through Google PageSpeed Insights. No data about your URL is stored on our servers. Enter your page URL below and click "Run Audit" to open the PageSpeed Insights report in a new tab. All analysis happens on Google's side and the report is between you and Google.
