Horrorcore Music

Horrorcore is a subgenre of hip hop that fuses rap with horror-themed lyrics, occult imagery, slasher-film aesthetics, and macabre storytelling. Originating in the early 1990s with pioneers like Gravediggaz, Flatlinerz, and Esham, horrorcore has evolved into a diverse underground movement spanning boom-bap, trap metal, and experimental soundscapes. Artists in the horrorcore scene often perform behind masks, embrace dark alter egos, and build entire mythologies around their music. The genre covers themes of psychological horror, supernatural terror, social decay, and personal darkness, delivered with an intensity and theatricality that sets it apart from mainstream hip hop.
