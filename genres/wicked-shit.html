Wicked Shit Music

Wicked shit is the catch-all term we use for the genre-defying, experimental fringe of the dark underground. These are artists who refuse categorization — blending elements of post-punk, darkwave, shoegaze, noise, and hip hop into sounds that exist in their own lane. Artists like Elita and Moonvampire represent the spirit of wicked shit: unpredictable, deeply personal, and impossible to box into a single genre label. If it is dark, strange, and genuinely independent, it belongs here.
