PRIVACY POLICY

LOCAL FILE HANDLING

All promotional tools (Flyer Builder, QR Overlay, Image Upscaler) process your images locally within your browser's memory. We do not upload, store, or host any user-provided images or promotional materials on our servers.

DATA AUDITS

Page audits utilize public APIs (such as Google PageSpeed Insights). We do not track or store the URLs you audit. Your promotional strategies remain private.

PUSH NOTIFICATIONS

If you choose to "Follow" an artist, we store an anonymized push token and the Artist ID in our database to send you alerts. You can unsubscribe at any time through your browser settings.
